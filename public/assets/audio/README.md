# Audio Files

Place your voice-over audio files here.

## Required Audio Files (referenced in story.json):

- `scene1.mp3` - Scene 1 voice-over
- `scene2.mp3` - Scene 2 voice-over
- `scene3.mp3` - Scene 3 voice-over
- `scene4.mp3` - Scene 4 voice-over
- `scene5.mp3` - Scene 5 voice-over
- `win.mp3` - Win scene audio
- `lose.mp3` - Lose scene audio
- `gift-unlocked.mp3` - Gift unlocked audio
- `gift-locked.mp3` - Gift locked audio
- `gift-screen.mp3` - Gift screen audio

## Audio Format

- Format: MP3 (recommended) or WAV
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz (recommended)
- Bitrate: 128kbps or higher

The game will work without audio files - they're optional enhancements. Audio will auto-play when scenes load (subject to browser autoplay policies).
